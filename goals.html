My 90-Day Plan & Aspirations

You said I'll "learn more in 1 year with me than 4 years in a normal job." I'm ready to prove that. Here's my plan to absorb everything I can, create immediate value, and become the "1 person" you're looking for.

My First 90 Days as Your "1 Person"

My goal is to learn by doing, move fast, and make your life easier.

First 30 Days

Absorb, Analyze & Assist

  • Understand Your Content Strategy: Deep dive into past episodes and content performance. Analyze what resonates most with the audience and understand your unique voice and style.
  • Learn the Current Workflow: Map out the entire process from recording to distribution. Identify bottlenecks and areas where I can assist immediately with tasks like research, show notes drafting, or coordinating.
  • Identify High-Potential Moments: Listen actively to new recordings, flagging compelling stories, insights, or frameworks that could become standout content pieces.
  • Learn the Business: Be a sponge. I'll sit in on any meeting you'll have me, listen to your calls, and read every book you recommend. I'm here to learn "all things entrepreneurship."
First 60 Days

Propose Improvements & Contribute

  • Propose Workflow Optimizations: Based on my 30-day analysis, suggest 1-2 concrete ways to streamline the content creation or distribution process using simple tools or system tweaks.
  • Analyze YouTube Performance: Study your channel analytics (CTR, retention, etc.). Research best practices for thumbnails and titles in your niche and present findings.
  • Contribute to Content Ideas: Start actively contributing ideas for episode topics, guest suggestions, or interesting angles based on trends and audience feedback.
  • Propose 1 New Format: Pitch one new, simple content format (e.g., a "Best of the Week" compilation, a "Where are they now?" segment on a past idea, or a text-based summary of key frameworks).
First 90 Days

Become a Reliable Partner

  • Understand the Full Picture: Demonstrate a thorough grasp of the entire podcast operation, from strategy and recording to content repurposing and community engagement.
  • Be Proactive & Reliable: Anticipate needs and reliably execute assigned tasks, freeing up your time to focus on high-level activities. Become a trusted assistant you don't need to micromanage.
  • Become an Idea Partner: I'll come to you once a week with 3 new, well-researched ideas. This could be a new guest, a business to break down, a trend to analyze, or a person to meet. I'll be your "young, hungry" idea generator.
  • Assist with Strategic Projects: Begin contributing to larger projects, whether it's analyzing new platform opportunities, researching potential sponsors, or helping outline content strategy.

Long-Term Aspirations

Beyond the first 90 days, my ambitions are focused on deep expertise, leadership, and a well-rounded life.

Become a World-Class Media Operator: My primary goal is to master the *entire* media game, from content strategy and production to audience growth and monetization. I want to learn how to build a media brand from the inside.

Learn Entrepreneurship by Osmosis: I'm here to learn entrepreneurship by being in the room. I want to be a fly on the wall, absorb how you think, and "meet awesome people" and build a network of winners.

Grow with the Brand: I'm not just here for a 1-year "tour." I'm applying because I want to grow with you and your brand for the long term. I'm ready to become the next Steven Bartlett or Ishan Haque on your "turned out alright" list.

Build a Family: Outside of my professional ambitions, a core part of my life's vision is to get married and raise a family of 3-5 kids, building a strong and loving home.

Maintain Health & Vitality: I am blessed with excellent health, and I am committed to maintaining it. Staying in peak physical and mental condition is crucial for performing at a high level in all areas of my life.

Key Areas I'm Hungry to Learn

These are the specific skills I am most excited to develop by working directly with you.

Effective Storytelling & Content Packaging

How to identify the most compelling 'hook' or insight within a conversation and understand the principles behind packaging it for maximum impact across different platforms.

Audience & Community Building

How to do more than just get views—how to build a loyal, engaged community that trusts you. I want to learn the 'back end' of the creator economy.
